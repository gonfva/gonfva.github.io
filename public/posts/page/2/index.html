AWS CloudWatch alarms

One of the things less obvious for anyone trying to setup Cloudwatch alarms is the treatment of missing points. You create an alarm, to detect too many 5xx errors in your ELB. But you don’t want it to be triggered too much, so you ask for at least one error per minute during 5 minutes. And you discover that the error gets triggered whenever during the last 5 minutes there was either zero, 2 or more.
