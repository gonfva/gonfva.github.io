What recruiters taught me about looking for a job

There are good recruiters. And there are bad recruiters. The good ones help you to find a job. The bad ones teach you how to look for a job. If you are looking for a job, be sure to do just the opposite of what the bad ones do. Almost every other day I receive an email offering me a position. "Good for you".  No, bad for me. Most of the emails come from people I haven't spoken to.
