--- layout: post title: "The passionate programmer" date: 2010-11-14 17:01:56 UTC updated: 2010-11-14 17:01:56 UTC comments: false categories: Personal --- The last few weeks have been really intense with respect to my career. I've attended two courses, given some presentations, launched a few projects at work, and read some books. Sometimes I've been under a lot of self-imposed pressure, but overall I've learned a lot of things.

One of the books I've read has been really enlightening. It has been The passionate Programmer. It's a book I wish I'd read years ago, and I'm tempted to buy a few copies and give them to some of my coworkers.

Initially I thought it was to late for me, but as times passes, I'm more and more convinced that it's not too late. I don't know what future will bring but I hope I'm still able to influence it.
